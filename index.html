Our Story

The Platform We Wish We Had Growing Up

Our journey began with a vision to create a platform shaped by real experiences and dreams, built to inspire and support you on your path to success.

Idea

It all started with a vision: to create a transparent, secure, and accessible platform for both investors and mentors. In 2023, Slice was born from the idea of bridging the gap between aspiring investors and professionals investors, offering a structured mentorship system for trading.

2023

Market Launch

After months of development and perfecting the platform, Slice officially launched in the English-speaking market in May 2024. This marked the beginning of a new era in trading mentorship, connecting mentors with a community of investors eager to learn and grow.

May 2024

Funding Milestone

In September 2024, Slice successfully raised over $1,000,000 in seed round. This moment has allowed us to accelerate our growth, improve our platform, and expand our reach, further empowering investors and mentors worldwide.
